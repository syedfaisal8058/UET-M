Vice Chancellor Message

Dear students, faculty, staff and visitors, Welcome to University of Engineering and Technology, Mardan. UET Mardan has come a long way since its inception and has established itself as a leading institution in the field of engineering and technology. We take pride in our commitment to academic excellence, research, and innovation, and our mission to produce graduates who can make a positive impact in society.

At UET Mardan, we are constantly striving to create an environment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills among our students. Our faculty is a dedicated team of experts who work tirelessly to ensure that our students receive the best education possible. Our research initiatives are focused on finding solutions to the most pressing issues facing our world today.

Our students have access to state-of-the-art facilities, including well-equipped laboratories, modern classrooms, and a library that houses a vast collection of books and research papers. We also offer a range of extracurricular activities to help our students develop their leadership and teamwork skills.

Our University has already established itself as a leader in the fields of telecommunication, electrical, computer software, civil, mechanical engineering, as well as computer science, mathematics, and English literature and linguistics programs, with an impressive legacy of five years. All of our undergraduate engineering programs are accredited by Pakistan Engineering Council (PEC) under the Outcome Based Education (OBE) system of the Washington Accord (WA). Our engineers with OBE degrees are recognized as being on par with the engineers of WA signatories and are sought after by employers in USA, Australia, Canada, New Zealand, Ireland, South Korea, China, Japan, UK, and Malaysia. Furthermore, our BS Computer Science program is accredited by the National Computing Education Accreditation Council (NCEAC).

As the Vice Chancellor of UET Mardan, I am committed to ensuring that our institution continues to grow and evolve to meet the changing needs of our society. We are constantly exploring new avenues for collaboration with leading universities and research institutions around the world to offer our students opportunities for global exposure and learning.

I invite you to explore our website and learn more about the programs we offer, the research we are engaged in, and the vibrant community that makes up UET Mardan. Whether you are a prospective student, a current student, a faculty member, or a member of our staff, I am confident that you will find our institution to be a place of learning, innovation, and growth.
